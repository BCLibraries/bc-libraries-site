Dear Member of the Boston College Community,

As you may know, in 2016 the Boston College Libraries are providing access to the Scopus database. It is similar in many ways to the Web of Science. Both databases can be accessed from the Boston College Libraries' Databasespage.

Both are multi-disciplinary databases that offer:

  • Wide-ranging exploratory research searching
  • Identification of potential collaborators at Boston College and beyond
  • Scholarly productivity analysis, such as H-Index analysis
  • Journal "impact" comparison tools, which can be useful in identifying potential journal publication targets
  • Ability to discover funding sources

We ask for your help with the goal of identifying the best resources for Boston College. Please do that by completing the attached survey by April 1st, which we anticipate will take 5 to 10 minutes.

If you have any questions or feedback, please don't hesitate to contact your subject specialist, or email the members of the task force charged with this investigation (scopustf-ggroup@bc.edu); they have set up a feedback form, which I would encourage you to use.

With my continuing best wishes,

Thomas B. Wall
