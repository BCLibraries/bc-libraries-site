Online Resources

Current Boston College Faculty, Students, and Staff can access Online Resources.

BC Login

Your BC username is the same username that you use to log into the BC Portal (username@bc.edu). Don't enter @bc.edu.

Can't Login?

  • Forgot your password? Contact the Help Center at 617-552-4357 or help.center@bc.edu. If you change your password, it can take up to 24 hours before your new password will work. Try your old password until it is updated.
  • Unregistered students won't be able to sign in and should contact Student Services.

Need Help?

Contact: eresources@bc.edu or complete the Online Access Problem form or call the O'Neill Library Reference Desk at 617-552-4472.
