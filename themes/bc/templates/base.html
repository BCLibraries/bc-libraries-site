      Libraries

      Bapst Art Library

      The Bapst Art Library, named for the first president of Boston College, served as the original Library from 1925 until the opening of the Thomas P. O'Neill Library in 1984.

      • Contact Info

      • (617) 552-3200

      • bapst@bc.edu

      • Today's Hours

      • All Library Hours

      Burns Library

      The University’s special collections, including the University’s Archives, are housed in the Honorable John J. Burns Library, located in the Bapst Library Building, north entrance.

      • Contact Info

      • (617) 552-4861

      • burns@bc.edu

      • Today's Hours

      • All Library Hours

      Educational Resource Center

      The Educational Resource Center, located in Campion Hall, serves the specialized resource needs of the Lynch School of Education faculty and students. In doing so, the ERC aims to further the broader missions of the LSOE faculty, and the University generally, in the area of teaching, research, and service.

      • Contact Info

      • (617) 552-4920

      • erc@bc.edu

      • Today's Hours

      • All Library Hours

      Law Library

      The mission of the Boston College Law Library is to provide research, educational and technological support to the Boston College Law School community.

      • Contact Info

      • (617) 552-0000

      • whoknows@bc.edu

      • Today's Hours

      • All Library Hours

      Catherine O'Connor Library

      Located at Weston Observatory, this library contains a specialized collection of earth sciences monographs, periodicals, and maps, particularly in the areas of seismology, geology, and geophysics.

      • Contact Info

      • (617) 552-4477

      • whoknows@bc.edu

      • Today's Hours

      • By appointment

      • All Library Hours

      O'Neill Library

      The main research library, the Thomas P. O'Neill, Jr. Library, is located in the center of the main campus, adjoining the O'Neill Plaza.

      • Contact Info

      • Circ: (617) 552-8038

      • Ref: (617) 552-4472

      • Today's Hours

      • All Library Hours

      Social Work Library

      Located in McGuinn Hall, the Social Work Library supports the teaching and research needs of the Graduate School of Social Work.

      • Contact Info

      • (617) 552-0109

      • Today's Hours

      • All Library Hours

      Theology & Ministry Library

      The Theology & Ministry Library (TML) supports research, teaching, and learning at the School of Theology & Ministry and Saint John's Seminary.
